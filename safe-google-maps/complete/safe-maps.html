Safer Google Maps

When a user uses Google Maps to get walking directions from one place to the next, Google Maps returns to the user the fastest route(s) that get the user to their destination. However, the app currently does not take into account safety, a factor that can be especially important when walking through giant cities like SF. Let's try and fix that!

Your job will be to return the safest walking directions when the user clicks on the "Get Safest Directions" button. To do this, we will be pulling from SF crime data from 2013-2015. To begin, refer to safe-maps.js!


To use the app below, click on the map with your mouse to indicate a start and end point, and then click on any of the buttons below to get either the fastest walking directions or the safest walking directions.
